All-in-One Solar Street Light

An integrated solar street light for municipal roads, rural pathways and parking lots — 100/200/300W, IP66, microwave radar detection and dusk-to-dawn automation in a single sealed unit.

JC Lightning · Shenzhen headquarters·CE + RoHS·OEM / ODM

This is the product that lets a town or developer light a road without a power grid. Everything — panel, LiFePO4 battery, LED and radar controller — is integrated into one housing that mounts on a standard pole in minutes. Microwave radar dims the light between passers-by and brings it to full output for vehicles and pedestrians, stretching runtime through long nights and cloudy spells.
