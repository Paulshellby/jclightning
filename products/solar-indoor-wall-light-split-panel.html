Solar Indoor Wall Light (Split Panel)

An indoor solar light designed for homes that live with outages — the panel mounts outside, the light goes inside on a 5m cable, and a USB port charges phones when the grid is down.

JC Lightning · Shenzhen headquarters·CE + RoHS·OEM / ODM

This product is purpose-built for the reality of load-shedding in African and Latin American homes. The solar panel mounts outside in the sun; a 5m cable runs the light indoors where it's needed. When the grid fails, the room stays lit and the built-in USB keeps phones charged — no generator, no candles. At 400 lumens with a 4000mAh LiFePO4 battery, it covers a living room or kitchen through an evening outage.
