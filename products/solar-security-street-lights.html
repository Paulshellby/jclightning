Security lighting is the category where reliability matters most — and where grid-tied fixtures fail exactly when they're needed. During load-shedding in Southern Africa or apagones in Latin America, a mains floodlight goes dark the moment the power cuts. A solar fixture doesn't: each unit is its own off-grid system.

JC Lightning's security & street range covers residential perimeters, gates and driveways, municipal roads, and construction sites — with motion detection, dusk-to-dawn operation, and the build quality to survive years outdoors.

Why buyers choose this range

LiFePO4 as standard. Every fixture above 1,000 lumens ships with a lithium iron phosphate battery — 2,000+ cycles, –20°C to +60°C, no fire risk. Why the battery chemistry matters →

The right IP rating. Security floods and street lights ship at IP66 for high-pressure water and dust; wall and garden units at IP65. IP65 vs IP66 vs IP67 explained →

Real motion detection. PIR sensors (8–12 m) on flood and wall units; microwave radar on street lights for vehicle and pedestrian detection at range.

Certified & OEM-ready. CE + RoHS through accredited labs, certificates provided with every quotation. Custom logo, colour, packaging and spec adjustments available.
