Solar Outdoor Floor Lamp

A freestanding solar floor lamp for patios and terraces — 60/90/120cm in aluminium and tempered glass, 24W and 1800 lumens of warm 3000K light, IP65, with dusk-to-dawn and an optional PIR.

JC Lightning · Shenzhen headquarters·CE + RoHS·OEM / ODM
