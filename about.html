About JC Lightning

A sourcing partner
for distributors in grid-failure markets.

JC Lightning sources solar outdoor lighting from verified Chinese factories — specifically for distributors, installers, and project contractors in Africa, Latin America, and Oceania.

Who We Are

We're not a factory. We're not a generic trader.

JC Lightning is a sourcing specialist focused on solar outdoor lighting. We bridge the gap between Chinese manufacturers and international distributors — handling factory selection, technical verification, CE documentation, and full export logistics.

"We handle the China side. You receive the goods."

Why this matters: Sourcing solar lighting directly from China is hard. There are thousands of factories. Many CE certificates are unverified. LiFePO4 batteries are often relabeled lithium-ion. Specifications are inflated. Export documentation is complex. We solve those problems on your behalf.

Why solar lighting: Colombia faces a projected 3,100 GWh energy deficit by 2027. Ecuador already experiences up to 10 hours of daily outages. South Africa averages 6–8 hours of load-shedding. In these markets, solar outdoor lighting is not an upgrade — it is a necessity. We focus exclusively on this category because the demand is real, growing, and structural.

What We Do

Four things we handle for you.

Everything between the factory floor in China and the container on your dock — that's our scope of work.

01

Factory Selection

We work with a vetted network of factories in Shenzhen, Zhongshan, and Guangzhou — production hubs that account for the majority of China's solar lighting exports. We only source from factories that pass our checks on documentation, materials, and reliability.

02

Technical Verification

We verify CE certificates come from accredited laboratories — not paper certificates. We confirm LiFePO4 cells are authentic, not relabeled lithium-ion. We check actual lumen output, IP ratings, and runtime against catalogue specifications before any quotation reaches you.

03

Multilingual Communication

English, Spanish, and Chinese. Latin American buyers can communicate in Spanish from initial enquiry through to final shipment. No translation gaps. No misunderstandings on technical specifications. No awkward English emails forwarded to overworked sales reps.

04

Export & Documentation

30% T/T deposit, factory production, pre-shipment inspection, balance payment, container loading, full export documentation: B/L, Certificate of Origin, CE certificate, packing list, commercial invoice. FOB Shenzhen/Yantian/Guangzhou. CIF and DDP arrangements on request.
