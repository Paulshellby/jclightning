Our Approach

Light That Works When the Power Doesn't

"The grid is failing markets across Africa, Latin America, and Oceania. Solar lighting isn't an upgrade — it's the answer."

By JC Lightning Engineering & Export Team·3 min read·Updated Jul 2026

The problem: the grid can't keep up

Colombia faces a projected 3,100 GWh energy deficit by 2027. Ecuador already suffers cuts of up to 10 hours a day. South Africa averages 6–8 hours of load-shedding daily. Across the markets that matter most for outdoor lighting, grid power is no longer something a distributor can assume — and grid-tied fixtures fail exactly when security lighting is needed most: after dark, during extended outages.

The answer: light that doesn't depend on the grid

Solar security, garden, decorative, home and portable lighting — built so your clients never go dark. Each unit captures sunlight by day and powers itself overnight, independent of whatever the grid is doing. For markets built on unstable power, solar lighting isn't a premium option; it is the functional category.

Who we are

JC Lightning Technology Co., Ltd. supplies outdoor solar lighting products to distributors, installation companies, project contractors and beyond — focused on markets where grid power supply is unstable. OEM and ODM available: logo, packaging and spec tuning to fit your brand and your market.

The proof: certified with every quotation

Quality specification is the differentiator, not price. The products that generate returns and repeat orders are those with certified components and battery chemistry that survives heat. Every quotation ships with the paperwork to back it — not chased after the fact.

CE

CE Certification

EMC, LVD, and RoHS compliance — certified by accredited third-party laboratories. Required for EU and most international markets.

RoHS

RoHS Compliance

Restriction of Hazardous Substances — lead-free, mercury-free, cadmium-free. Test reports available for every product series.

IP65

IP65 / IP66 Rated

Dust-tight and protected against water jets. Independently tested. Security range minimum IP65 — verified before shipment.

LiFePO₄

LiFePO₄ Battery

2,000+ charge cycles. Thermal stable from -20°C to 60°C. No thermal runaway risk. 3× longer lifespan than standard lithium-ion.

Bottom line: Where the grid is unreliable, solar lighting is the answer — and specification discipline is what makes it last. CE + RoHS certified, LiFePO4, IP65/IP66, with OEM/ODM flexibility and certificates provided upfront with every quotation.
