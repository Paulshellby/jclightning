### Post 5 · Day 10 — 电池技术对比

**栏目:** 教育 | **配图:** 左右对比图 — 红侧标"普通Li-ion 500次"，绿侧标"LiFePO4 2000+次"

#### EN

```
A solar light that dies in 12 months costs more than one that lasts 5 years.

The difference? What's inside the battery compartment.

Standard Li-ion (what most cheap solar lights use):
  ▸ 300–500 charge cycles
  ▸ Degrades fast above 35°C
  ▸ Voltage sags as it drains — light gets dimmer through the night

LiFePO4 (what we use, no exceptions):
  ▸ 2,000+ charge cycles — roughly 5–7 years of daily use
  ▸ Stable from –20°C to +60°C — built for Colombian coast, South African summer
  ▸ Flat discharge curve — same brightness at 11pm as at 7pm
  ▸ No thermal runaway — won't overheat, won't catch fire

Every JC Lightning security product ships with LiFePO4. Not as an upgrade. Not as a "premium tier." Standard.

Because a light your customer has to replace after one season isn't a product — it's a complaint waiting to happen.

👇 Request a spec sheet. Check the battery section yourself.
📞 +86 131 3818 9642
```
