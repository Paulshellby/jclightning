Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect the information you share with JC Lightning.

JC Lightning (Shenzhen Jiachen Lighting Technology Co., Ltd.)·Last updated 13 June 2026

JC Lightning (“we”, “us”, “our”) manufactures and exports solar lighting from Shenzhen, China. This policy explains what information we collect through this website, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to jclightning.com and the enquiry channels linked from it.

Information we collect

We only collect the information you choose to give us. We do not run user accounts, and we do not take payments on this website.

  • Enquiry form — your name, email address, and (optionally) your company, product interest, and message details such as product type, quantity, and destination.
  • Direct contact — if you reach us by WhatsApp or email, we receive the contact details and message content you send.

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal data, and we ask that you do not send it through these channels.

How we use your information

We use the information solely to:

  • respond to your enquiry and prepare quotations;
  • communicate with you about products, samples, orders, and shipping;
  • maintain our ongoing business relationship with you.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising or automated profiling.

How your information is handled and shared

Enquiry-form submissions are processed by FormSubmit (formsubmit.co), a third-party service that forwards your message to our company email. Your message is also stored in our email and business systems so our team can follow up. We share your information only with service providers that help us operate (such as the form processor and email host), and only as needed to respond to you — or where required by law.

Cookies and local storage

At present this website uses only functional browser storage — for example, remembering your chosen language and whether you have already seen the intro animation. We do not currently use advertising or analytics tracking cookies. If we add website analytics in future, we will update this policy and the cookie notice accordingly.

Data retention

We keep enquiry correspondence for as long as needed to serve your request and our business relationship, and to meet legal or accounting obligations. You may ask us to delete your information at any time (see below).

Your rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, or ask us to stop contacting you. To do so, email us at the address below and we will respond within a reasonable time.

International transfers

We operate from China, so information you send us is processed there and by the service providers noted above. By contacting us, you understand that your information may be transferred to and handled in these locations.

Children’s privacy

This is a business-to-business website and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “last updated” date at the top reflects the latest version.

Contact us

For any privacy question or request, contact:

  • JC Lightning (Shenzhen Jiachen Lighting Technology Co., Ltd.), Shenzhen, Guangdong, China
  • Email: l488789496@outlook.com

Questions about working with us?

Tell us your market and requirements — we’ll reply with product recommendations and a quotation.
