Request a Specification Sheet
01

LiFePO4 Battery Specification

Battery quality is where most solar products fail in the field. We specify LiFePO4 chemistry — not standard lithium-ion — across our security and pillar light range. Rated capacity and internal resistance are confirmed before acceptance. Your clients receive lights that hold their stated output through 2,000+ discharge cycles, not products that fade within a season.

02

Built for African & Pacific Conditions

Product selection is driven by the conditions your clients operate in: high UV index, temperatures from 5°C to 45°C, unreliable grid supply, and the physical security demands of properties across Southern Africa and Oceania. Battery capacity, panel wattage, housing material, and IP rating are all specified accordingly — not chosen for a showroom.

03

Factory-Direct Partnerships

We source through established factory partnerships in Zhongshan and the Pearl River Delta — China's core outdoor lighting manufacturing region. Direct relationships give us access to specification-level negotiation, pre-shipment inspection, and consistent pricing without distributor markups. Trial order minimums of 200 pcs allow you to validate quality before scaling.

04

Certifications You Can Verify

CE and RoHS documentation is provided with every product quotation — including the issuing laboratory, test report reference number, and date of issue. Every certificate is independently verifiable. We do not self-declare compliance, list certifications we cannot produce on request, or recycle documents from unrelated product lines.
