Discuss Your Project
01

15+ Years of In-House Manufacturing

Founded in 2009. 8,000 m² facility. 200+ production staff. Dedicated R&D engineering team. PCB drivers designed, LiFePO4 packs assembled, and photometric + waterproof tests conducted entirely in-house.

02

LiFePO4 Battery Integration

Every cell tested for actual capacity and internal resistance before assembly. LiFePO4 delivers 2,000+ charge cycles, zero memory effect, and stable discharge from –20°C to +60°C — double the lifespan of standard lithium batteries.

03

Full OEM & ODM Capability

Custom housing design, private label branding, PCB modification, bespoke packaging. Concept to prototype in 30 days. Prototype to mass production in 45–60 days.

04

Third-Party Verified Certifications

CE and RoHS certificates issued by accredited labs — test report number and date included. Independently verifiable. No self-declared compliance. No recycled certificates from unrelated products.
