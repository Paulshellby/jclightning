Common Questions

Answers before you ask.

Do your products work during power cuts?

Yes — that's the point. Every solar product charges independently during daylight and operates through the night without any connection to the grid. In markets like Colombia, Ecuador, and South Africa where outages are frequent or extended, this is the core selling argument to your end customers.

What is your typical MOQ?

From 200 units for trial orders on most products, scaling to 1,000+ for retail-pack programmes. We work with SOHO buyers, small distributors, and large importers alike.

What payment terms do you accept?

30% T/T deposit and 70% before shipment is standard. Letter of Credit (L/C at sight) accepted for orders above USD 30,000. Custom terms negotiable for repeat clients.

What is the standard lead time?

30–45 days for standard products. 45–60 days for OEM and custom packaging. Sea freight adds 25–40 days depending on destination port.

Do you provide samples?

Yes. Sample fees typically USD 50–150 per unit including international DHL or FedEx shipping. Sample cost is fully refunded against your first order.

Can you handle OEM and ODM?

Yes. Custom finishes, private-label packaging, brand printing, and PCB modification all available. MOQ for custom branding starts at 500–1,000 units.

What certifications come with your products?

CE and RoHS as standard, with documentation issued by accredited laboratories. FCC, SAA, IC, and other regional certifications available on request.

Which ports do you ship from?

Shenzhen (Yantian), Guangzhou (Nansha), and Shanghai are our standard FOB ports. CIF and DDP arrangements also available on request.
