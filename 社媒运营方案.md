**EN：**
```
Why do most cheap solar lights die within a year?

The battery.

Most budget solar lights use standard Li-ion batteries. In high heat (Colombian coast, South African summer), these degrade fast. 300-500 cycles and they're done.

LiFePO4 (lithium iron phosphate) is different:
- 2,000+ charge cycles — 4x the lifespan
- Stable from -20°C to +60°C
- No thermal runaway risk
- Consistent discharge — the light stays bright all night

Every JC Lightning security product uses LiFePO4. No exceptions.

Cheap battery = cheap light that lasts one season.
LiFePO4 = a product your customers will reorder.

👇 Request a spec sheet — check the battery specs yourself.
```

## 六、AI 建团队实操

### 角色 1：内容写手 → Claude / ChatGPT
每周批量生成 5-7 条帖子草稿，给出产品信息 + 目标市场 → 出双语文案。

**核心 Prompt 模板：**
> You're a B2B copywriter for a solar lighting exporter. Write 3 Facebook posts this week:
> 1. Product spotlight: [产品名 + 规格]
> 2. Market insight: [目标市场痛点]
> 3. Educational: [技术知识点]
> Target audience: importers and distributors in LatAm and Africa. Tone: professional, direct, no hype. Include relevant hashtags.

### 角色 2：图片设计 → Canva + Midjourney/DALL-E
- Canva：产品对比图、规格说明图、品牌 quote 卡
- Midjourney/DALL-E：场景化图片（如"夜晚非洲社区被太阳能灯照亮"）
- **优先实拍**：尽快用手机拍一组工厂/产品/包装场景照片，实拍 > AI 生成

### 角色 3：排期发布 → Meta Business Suite
- 免费，FB 官方工具
- 一次排一周内容，FB 和 IG 同步发布
- 查看数据：reach、engagement、link clicks
- 设置自动回复常见问题

### 角色 4：客户响应 → WhatsApp Business
- 下载 WhatsApp Business App
- 设置非工作时间自动回复
- 快捷回复模板：价格问询、样品流程、MOQ
- 标签分类：按国家/产品兴趣打标签

### 每日运营流程（30-45 分钟）

| 时间段 | 动作 | 工具 |
|--------|------|------|
| 早上 | 检查消息（WhatsApp + FB Inbox），回复询盘 | WhatsApp Business |
