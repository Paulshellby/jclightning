## 二、群内日常互动话术

### 场景 1：有人问"哪里找太阳能灯供应商？"

**不要直接推销**，先提供价值：

```
We supply solar outdoor lighting from Shenzhen — security, garden, and decorative ranges. But rather than pitch you, here's what to look for in any supplier:

✅ LiFePO4 battery (not standard Li-ion) — confirms it lasts 2,000+ cycles
✅ IP65 minimum — critical for tropical and coastal markets
✅ CE + RoHS certificates provided WITH the quotation, not after
✅ Cast aluminium housing, not plastic
✅ Pre-shipment inspection report before final payment

If a supplier can't confirm all five, keep looking. Happy to share what we've learned shipping to 30+ markets — DM open.
```

### 场景 2：有人讨论南非/哥伦比亚市场

```
Re: [Market] — we've shipped a few containers there. Security PIR wall lights move fastest, followed by solar floodlights. Key is LiFePO4 battery — the heat destroys standard Li-ion within a season. Also, they want the CE certificate upfront, not "we'll send it later."

DM me if you want market-specific product recs. Not selling, just sharing data.
```


### 模板 2：技术分享帖

```
Quick PSA for anyone importing solar outdoor lights:

Not all "solar lights" are built the same. Three things to check on every spec sheet:

1. Battery chemistry — LiFePO4, not standard Li-ion. The difference is 500 cycles vs 2,000+ cycles. In tropical heat, this matters more than anything else.

2. Housing material — Cast aluminium, not ABS plastic. Aluminium dissipates heat, plastic traps it. Heat kills batteries.

3. IP rating — IP65 minimum for outdoor. IP44 or IP54 means dust and moisture WILL get in over time.

We include all three as standard across our range. But even if you don't buy from us — check these three specs with whoever you do buy from.

Your customers will thank you a year later.
```

## 六、群组互动策略

### 第一周：观察
- 每天花 10 分钟浏览群内帖子
- 记录：什么内容互动高？什么时间发帖多？谁是这个群的活跃人物？
- **不要在第一天就自我介绍**

### 第二周：互动
- 在别人的帖子下留有价值的评论（技术问题、市场见解）
- 点赞活跃成员的帖子
- 不发自己的帖子，只回复

### 第三周：自我介绍 + 首帖
- 发自我介绍（用上面的模板）
- 发第一条有价值的内容帖（行业洞察或技术分享）
- **不要配产品链接**，纯干货

### 第四周起：规律发布
- 每周在每个群发 1 条内容帖（不要多，避免被认为是 spam）
- 持续在他人帖下互动
- 有人问供应商相关问题，自然提及自己的产品

### 核心原则
- **80/20 法则：** 80% 互动 + 提供价值，20% 提到自己
- **先给后拿：** 回答别人问题 → 建立信誉 → 自然获得询盘
- **不要直接发产品广告**，会被踢
- **DM 要礼貌：** 先说明你在哪个群看到对方，再问是否有需求
