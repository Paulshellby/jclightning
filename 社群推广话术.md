## 二、群内互动 · 四类场景

### 场景 ① 有人问"哪里找太阳能灯供应商？"

**策略：** 不推自己，先给筛选标准。让提问者拿着标准去比——你的产品自然符合。

```
We supply solar outdoor lighting from Shenzhen. But instead of pitching you, here's what to check with any supplier:

☑ LiFePO4 battery, not standard Li-ion — confirms 2,000+ cycles, not 500
☑ IP65 minimum — non-negotiable for tropical and coastal markets
☑ CE + RoHS sent WITH the quotation — not "on request" later
☑ Cast aluminium or 304SS housing — if it's ABS plastic, walk
☑ Pre-shipment QC report before final payment — you see your goods before you pay

If a supplier hesitates on any of these five, keep looking. Happy to share what we've learned shipping to 30+ countries — DM open.
```

### 场景 ② 有人讨论南非 / 哥伦比亚 / 厄瓜多尔市场

**策略：** 用具体数据回应，展示你真的在那个市场出过货。

```
Re: [Market] — we've shipped there. Here's what moves:

→ Colombia / Ecuador: 3-head solar floodlights with PIR. Energy deficit is driving demand that won't peak until 2027-28. LiFePO4 is critical — coastal heat destroys standard batteries within 12 months.

→ South Africa: Wall-mounted PIR security lights dominate. Load-shedding made them the default outdoor fitting. Distributors want CE certificate upfront, not "we'll send it later."

→ Spain: Warm-white decorative wall lanterns. Dual-mode solar/AC. Design finish matters as much as the lumen count.

DM me if you want product recommendations for your specific market. Not selling — sharing data.
```

### 场景 ③ 有人问技术问题（电池 / 防水 / 认证）

**策略：** 直接给硬核答案。三句话建立专业印象。

```
On [battery type / IP rating / certification]:

[1–2 sentence factual answer — be specific, not generic]

We standardise on [spec] across all products. The reason: [one sentence on why — durability / safety / compliance].

If you're comparing suppliers, ask for [specific detail on the spec sheet]. That's where corners get cut — and where the difference shows after 12 months in the field.

Happy to share a sample spec sheet so you know what a complete one looks like.
```

### 场景 ④ 有人抱怨现有供应商质量

**策略：** 不问"要不要换供应商"，先共情再提供参考标准。

```
That's frustrating. Unfortunately common with outdoor solar — the battery and the housing are where most suppliers cut cost.

Two things that might help even if you stay with your current supplier:
1. Ask for the battery cell manufacturer on the spec sheet — not just "Li-ion" but whose cells
2. Request the IP test certificate, not just the label on the housing

If you ever want a second set of eyes on a spec sheet, I'm happy to look. We manufacture in this category — I can tell you pretty quickly if the specs hold up.

DM open.
```

## 三、群内主动发帖 · 两套模板

> 每周每群最多 1 条。不配链接。不发产品图。纯干货。

### 模板 A · 市场趋势短报

```
Quick market note for anyone importing solar lighting ↓

🇨🇴🇪🇨 Colombia / Ecuador: 3-head solar floodlights with PIR are the fastest-moving category. Energy deficit projections (3,100 GWh by 2027 in Colombia) mean demand will only increase.

🇿🇦 South Africa: Wall-mounted PIR security lights dominate residential and commercial. Load-shedding made solar the default — the grid alternative barely exists now.

🇪🇸🇦🇺 Spain / Australia: Garden and decorative. Warm white. Material finish (304SS, cast aluminium) matters as much as brightness. Buyers in these markets read spec sheets.

We ship to all five countries. If you're stocking for any of these markets and want to compare notes on what specific specs move best, DM me.
```

### 模板 B · 技术避坑指南

```
Three specs to check on every solar outdoor light — whether you buy from us or not:

1. Battery chemistry
   LiFePO4 = 2,000+ cycles. Standard Li-ion = 300–500. In 35°C+ heat (Colombian coast, African summer), Li-ion degrades fast. LiFePO4 was designed for this.

2. Housing material
   Cast aluminium dissipates heat. ABS plastic traps it. A plastic housing in direct sun is an oven for the battery inside. The battery dies. The light dies.

3. IP rating
   IP65 minimum for outdoor. IP44 or IP54 = dust and moisture ingress over time. If the spec sheet says "water-resistant" instead of giving a number, that's a red flag.

We build all three into every product. But the point is: check these three on whoever's spec sheet you're reading. Your customers will feel the difference a year later.
```

## 六、四周渐进策略

> 核心原则：80% 互动 + 提供价值，20% 提到自己。先给后拿。

### Week 1 · 观察
- 每天 10 分钟浏览群内帖子
- 记录三件事：什么内容互动高？什么时间发帖多？谁是活跃人物？
- **不发帖，不自我介绍，只观察**

### Week 2 · 参与
- 在别人帖子下留有价值的评论（技术问题 / 市场见解）
- 点赞活跃成员的帖子
- 不发自己的帖，只回复
- **目标：群内成员开始认得你的名字**

### Week 3 · 亮身份
- 发自我介绍（用第一章模板）
- 发第一条干货帖（市场趋势或技术避坑，不用产品链接）
- 继续日常互动
- **目标：建立"懂行的人"这个印象**

### Week 4 起 · 规律运营
- 每周每群 ≤ 1 条内容帖（避免被当 spam）
- 持续在他人帖下互动
- 有人问供应商相关问题 → 自然提及自己产品
- **目标：每月 3–5 条有效询盘来自社群**

### 红线（触犯即踢群）
- 不粘贴产品链接
- 不发价格表图片
- 不在别人帖子下直接留自己的 WhatsApp
- 不用"Best quality, best price"这类空洞词
